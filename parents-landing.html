Young Rewired State

Young Rewired State is a national event from the 6th to 12th August. Your child will be invited to a week of learning and socialising alongside like-minded people from your local area. This will take place in local centres. At the end of the week, your child will be invited to the national festival of code on Saturday.

This website has been designed to be as useful as possible for parents. However, if there is something we haven't explained, feel free to contact us at any time.

Checklist

For the week

  • Transport to and from the centre each day

  • Possibly packed lunch or money for lunch

  • A laptop

For the Festival of Code

  • Funds to get to the site

  • A tent

  • Sleeping bag

  • Toiletries

  • Your contact details

Rules

Festival of Code Sleepover Rules

Please check back regularly, as these rules are subject to change before the event!

  • Age limit: 18 years and under (no lower age range)

  • Young people aged 18+ can camp with no parental supervision

  • Young people aged 16-18 can camp without parental supervision, so long as written agreement is provided to Rewired State

  • Young people aged 15 and under will need to be accompanied by a parent or carer
