Information for Parents

Young Rewired State

Young Rewired State is free for your child. It runs from the 6 → 12th August and we will aim to find a local centre for your child. If no local centre can be found, we will offer to mentor them remotely and they will be invited to come to the festival of code, and to the show and tell on the Saturday. We will be in touch with your child directly to send them information about the event, the centre that they have been allocated to and some resources for them to brush up their skills or acquire some basics. Each centre will be different, some will provide lunches and some will not — you will get detailed information about these practical things from the centre itself in June. You will be expected to cover the costs for getting your child to and from the centre each day.

The Friday and Saturday festival

This year all of the young people who take part in YRS are invited to spend the Friday (and Saturday) night at the festival of code. They will be sleeping in tents and coding, practising their presentations and so on — as well as all meeting each other and having a brilliant time. Each centre will organise the travel to the festival site and will go with the young people and will continue to work with them during the 24 hours they are there. If your child is being mentored remotely, the mentor will organise this with you. There will be a huge show and tell on the Saturday to a selection of press, government, industry, sponsors and people who want to support them — you are all welcome. There is no pre-requisite for the Saturday show and tell kids to have been at the camp over the night before, they can just come on the Saturday morning for the presentations.

Important: We will need to be alerted to any medical needs/allergies (centres will collect this information in June/July)

Further information

This year we have asked for a number of parents from previous years to act as points of contact for all of you — for any questions you may want to ask a parent who has had a child attend Young Rewired State before.

The contact details for these parents will be shared with you through your child (once they have registered) but in the first instance, please contact "Angi Long". To speak to one of the organisers about other practical matters, please use the contact details on this site.

Rules

Festival of Code Sleepover Rules

Please check back regularly, as these rules are subject to change before the event!

  • Age limit: 18 years and under (no lower age range)

  • Young people aged 18+ can camp with no parental supervision

  • Young people aged 16-18 can camp without parental supervision, so long as written agreement is provided to Rewired State

  • Young people aged 15 and under will need to be accompanied by a parent or carer

Who is Rewired State?

We run hack days. We take between 10 – 150 talented developers and give them money, time, space, caffeine, sugar and food, whilst they build cool/creative prototypes to solve your problems. If you'd like to kickstart a new project or accelerate an existing Research & Development programme, get in touch. Find out more about Rewired State →
